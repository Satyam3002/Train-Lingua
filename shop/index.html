About Us

Who We Are

Train Lingua was founded by passionate foreign language educators who have firsthand experience with the challenges in the industry. These include low salaries for trainers, misleading course duration promises by institutes, and exorbitant fees for students. Many institutes pressure trainers to rush through syllabi, which ultimately compromises both the quality of education and student outcomes.

Our Mission

To address these issues, we launched Train Lingua with a vision to create a fair and transparent learning ecosystem. Our goal is to:

  • Build trust with students by offering honest guidance, structured learning, and a student-first approach to language education.
  • Provide personalized career counseling, ensuring students receive the right guidance without misleading claims.
  • Ensure trainers receive fair compensation that reflects their qualifications and expertise, boosting their motivation and credibility.

What Makes Train Lingua Different?

  • Personalized Career Counseling – We assess each student’s career goals and recommend the best learning path.
  • Highly Qualified & Motivated Trainers – Our trainers are well-compensated, ensuring high-quality education and credibility.
  • Exam-Centric Preparation – We provide focused training for international language proficiency tests (DELF/DALF, TEF/TCF, DELE, etc.) using real exam papers.
  • Structured Learning Approach – Our well-defined course structure ensures timely syllabus completion and thorough revision.
  • Flexible & Customized Course Durations – Standard courses for effective learning, with customized plans based on individual needs.
  • Speaking Skills Enhancement – Interactive classroom activities to develop real-world conversational fluency.
  • Small Batch Sizes (3-7 Students) – Ensures personalized attention and an engaging learning environment.
  • Career Guidance & Industry Insights – Monthly seminars led by professionals offering insights into job opportunities related to language skills.
  • Global Networking & Mentorship – Online sessions with professionals and students abroad to guide those seeking international opportunities.

At Train Lingua, we are not just teaching languages—we are shaping futures. Join us for a transparent, structured, and results-driven language-learning journey!
